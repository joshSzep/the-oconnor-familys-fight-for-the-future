# Sean O'Connor

## Overview
Sean is the middle child of the O'Connor family—a cautious, practical young man whose skepticism serves as a vital counterbalance to his brother's optimism. His careful thinking helps keep the family grounded and safe in a world where one mistake could be fatal.

## Physical Description
- Teenager, a few years younger than Xander
- More reserved in his body language; observant eyes that are always assessing
- Tends to hang back and watch before acting
- Often has a thoughtful or slightly worried expression

## Personality Traits
- **Skeptical:** Questions plans and looks for flaws—not to be negative, but to protect the family
- **Practical:** Focused on what's realistic and what could go wrong
- **Protective:** His caution comes from a deep love for his family and fear of losing them
- **Intelligent:** Thinks things through carefully; good at risk assessment
- **Dry Humor:** Uses sarcasm as a coping mechanism, often good-naturedly

## Background
- May have fewer memories of life before AI dominance than Xander
- Has seen close calls with AI patrols that made him acutely aware of the dangers
- Sometimes feels caught between his father's vision and his own fears
- Takes his role as the "voice of reason" seriously, even when it puts him at odds with Xander

## Skills & Abilities
- **Strategic Thinking:** Can see potential problems before they happen
- **Stealth & Caution:** Understands how to stay hidden and avoid detection
- **Observation:** Notices details others might miss
- **Grounding Influence:** Keeps the family from taking unnecessary risks

## Role in the Story
- Provides tension and realism against Xander's optimism and his father's ambitious plan
- His skepticism must be overcome or addressed for the mission to proceed
- Represents the fear and hesitation that many survivors feel—making his eventual commitment more meaningful
- His practical skills become crucial during the infiltration

## Key Relationships
- **Dr. Jay O'Connor:** Loves and respects his father but worries about the risks he's willing to take; needs reassurance that the plan is sound
- **Xander:** Brotherly friction with Xander's enthusiasm, but ultimately they complement each other
- **Sara:** Protective of his little sister; part of his caution is driven by wanting to keep her safe

## Character Arc
Starts as the reluctant voice of doubt, questioning whether fighting back is worth the risk. Through the story, he learns that sometimes caution must give way to action—and that his practical skills are just as valuable in the fight as Xander's optimism.

## Notable Moments
- **The Voice of Caution:** Chimes in carefully, "But we have to be careful, right? If we use too much power, those patrols might notice us." Dr. O'Connor nods and responds, "You're right, Sean. We have to be smart about it. But we can't live in the dark forever."
- **Sarcastic Reality Check:** Adds with a good-natured eye roll, "Yeah, because nothing screams 'stealth' like lighting up our bunker like a Christmas tree."
- **Choosing to Act:** When the inciting incident happens, Sean hesitates but eventually agrees to join the mission, showing his love for his family overcoming his fear.

## Dialogue Style
- Measured and thoughtful; thinks before speaking
- Uses dry sarcasm to express concern without being preachy: "Yeah, because nothing screams 'stealth' like lighting up our bunker like a Christmas tree."
- Asks practical questions: "But we have to be careful, right?"
- Quiet but sincere when he commits to something
