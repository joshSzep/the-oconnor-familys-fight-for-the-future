# Sara O'Connor

## Overview
Sara is the youngest member of the O'Connor family—a bright, imaginative little girl whose innocence and sweetness remind everyone why they're fighting. In a world of darkness and danger, she represents hope and the future worth saving.

## Physical Description
- Young child, approximately 6-8 years old
- Bright, curious eyes that still see wonder in the world
- Often clutching a drawing, toy, or her father's hand
- Small but full of energy and expression

## Personality Traits
- **Exceptionally Intelligent:** Very smart for her age; picks up on things quickly and sometimes surprises adults with her understanding
- **Innocent:** Too young to fully understand the dangers they face, which is both a blessing and a concern
- **Imaginative:** Creates art and stories that envision a better world
- **Sweet:** Her kindness and love bring the family together in difficult moments
- **Surprisingly Witty:** Has a touch of humor beyond her years, likely picked up from her brothers
- **Hopeful:** Believes in her family completely and trusts that they'll make things better

## Background
- Born into or grew up entirely in the underground world; may have no real memories of life above
- Has been sheltered as much as possible from the harsh realities by her father and brothers
- Her drawings of "normal life" suggest she dreams of a world she's only heard stories about
- Brings unexpected lightness to the family dynamic

## Skills & Abilities
- **Emotional Intelligence:** Senses when her family is stressed and tries to help in her own way
- **Creativity:** Expresses hope and dreams through her drawings
- **Unity:** Her presence reminds the family what they're fighting for
- **Resilience:** Maintains her spirit despite growing up in hiding

## Role in the Story
- The heart of the family—everyone's motivation for fighting
- Her innocence contrasts sharply with the dark world, highlighting what's at stake
- May play a key emotional role during critical moments, grounding her father or brothers
- Represents the next generation and the future humanity is trying to reclaim

## Key Relationships
- **Dr. Jay O'Connor:** Daddy's girl; holds his hand when scared, and he's fiercely protective of her
- **Xander:** Looks up to her big brother; he plays along with her games and promises her a better future
- **Sean:** Also protective of her; part of his caution stems from wanting to keep her safe

## Character Arc
Sara's arc is less about personal change and more about her impact on others. Her presence, her drawings, and her simple faith in her family inspire the others to keep fighting. She may have a pivotal moment where her innocence cuts through fear or conflict.

## Notable Moments
- **The Drawing:** Runs up to show the family a picture she drew—the family above ground in a normal life. "I gave us all capes. Because obviously, we're all superheroes."
- **The Promise:** When shown her drawing, Xander says, "That's a beautiful picture, Sara. We'll get back to that kind of life someday." Dr. O'Connor adds, "We will, sweetheart. I promise."
- **Holding On:** During the inciting incident, Sara holds onto her dad's hand, showing the family coming together.

## Dialogue Style
- Simple and direct, as befitting her age
- Sweet with unexpected humor: "I gave us all capes. Because obviously, we're all superheroes."
- Asks innocent questions that sometimes cut to the heart of things
- Expresses love openly and without reservation
