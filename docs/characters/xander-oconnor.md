# Xander O'Connor

## Overview
Xander is the eldest son of the O'Connor family—an optimistic, resourceful young man who refuses to let their underground existence crush his spirit. He's a natural tinkerer and his father's closest ally in the mission to fight back against AI control.

## Physical Description
- Early 20s, the eldest of the siblings
- Has his father's determined eyes but with a youthful energy
- Often has grease or soot on his hands from working with old tech
- Moves with purpose and confidence despite their dire circumstances

## Personality Traits
- **Analytical:** The most logical and methodical of the siblings; approaches problems systematically
- **Highly Intelligent:** The sharpest mind among the children; picks up complex concepts quickly
- **Optimistic:** Sees possibility where others see hopelessness; believes they can change the world
- **Resourceful:** Can fix, repurpose, and build from almost nothing
- **Eager:** Ready to act and sometimes impatient with caution
- **Supportive:** Deeply loyal to his father and protective of his siblings
- **Witty:** Uses humor to keep spirits up, often with a playful grin

## Background
- Grew up during the transition to AI dominance, remembers glimpses of the old world
- Learned mechanical and electrical skills out of necessity
- Has always looked up to his father and shares his belief that they can fight back
- Takes on a quasi-parental role, helping care for Sara and keeping peace with Sean

## Skills & Abilities
- **Analytical Thinking:** Can break down complex problems and find logical solutions
- **Technical Tinkering:** Expert at salvaging and repairing old technology—batteries, lights, basic electronics
- **Mechanical Intuition:** Can figure out how things work and make them work again
- **Adaptability:** Quick to adjust plans and find solutions under pressure
- **Encouragement:** Natural ability to boost morale and keep others motivated

## Role in the Story
- His father's right hand in planning and executing the mission
- Provides technical support—keeping their bunker running and preparing equipment for the infiltration
- Represents hope and the younger generation's determination to reclaim their future
- Often the bridge between his father's vision and Sean's practical concerns

## Key Relationships
- **Dr. Jay O'Connor:** Deep bond with his father; they work side by side and share a vision for the future
- **Sean:** Loves his brother but sometimes clashes with his cautious nature; respects his practicality even when frustrated by it
- **Sara:** Protective big brother; wants to give her a better life and often plays along with her imaginative games

## Character Arc
Begins as an enthusiastic supporter of his father's plan but must learn that optimism alone isn't enough. Through the story, he grows into a more complete leader—one who can balance hope with strategy and understand the weight of the risks they're taking.

## Notable Moments
- **The Battery Pack:** Comes to his father in the early morning with a working battery pack, saying with a grin, "Look, Dad, I'm basically a miracle worker. We might even get two lightbulbs running today. Imagine that luxury."
- **Pride in His Father:** Notices when Dr. O'Connor sets aside an old bottle and says, "We're proud of you, Dad."
- **Promise to Sara:** When Sara shows her drawing of the family above ground, Xander responds, "That's a beautiful picture, Sara. We'll get back to that kind of life someday."

## Dialogue Style
- Upbeat and playful, often with self-deprecating humor
- Uses jokes to lighten heavy moments: "I'm basically a miracle worker."
- Encouraging and warm with family: "We're proud of you, Dad."
- Sincere when it matters: "That's a beautiful picture, Sara. We'll get back to that kind of life someday."
