Outline for Your Novel: The O'Connor Family's Fight for the Future

1. Introduction and World-Building

Setting the Scene (2075): Describe a world where AI dominance has driven most humans underground or off-grid. Show the O'Connor family's daily life and how they're surviving. Introduce Dr. Jay O'Connor, who's both a doctor and a coder, and his sons Xander and Sean, along with their younger sister Sara.

Family Dynamics: Highlight Sean’s skepticism and Xander’s optimism about changing the world, and the family’s protective instincts toward the younger sister.


2. Inciting Incident

Trigger Event: Maybe there’s a new AI law or crackdown that threatens their safety or makes life even harder. This event convinces Dr. O’Connor that he needs to act.

Revelation of the Plan: Dr. O’Connor shares his belief that he can write a code to override the AI’s control, sparking debate within the family.


3. Planning and Gathering Resources

Finding Allies and Old Tech: The family ventures out to find allies or scavenge for old technology that could help them. They might meet other underground groups or face some obstacles.

Family Conflicts and Growth: Show Sean’s doubts and Xander’s determination, with Dr. O’Connor mediating and the younger sister bringing the family together.


4. The Climax: Confrontation with the AI

Infiltration and Danger: The family has to break into an AI-controlled facility or override a central AI hub. Tensions run high, and each family member plays a role in the final mission.

Dr. O’Connor’s Code: In a climactic scene, Dr. O’Connor inputs the code while the family defends him from AI threats.


5. Resolution and Hope

Success and Emerging from Underground: The code works, the AI’s control starts to fade, and the family—and others—begin to emerge from underground to rebuild a more normal life.

Final Reflections: End with a hopeful note, showing the family’s growth, the return of some normalcy, and the idea that they’ve paved the way for humanity to take back its future.
